## Versioning Policy

- Major version changes, such as `2.0.0`, indicate substantial legal or operational changes.
- Minor version changes, such as `1.1.0`, indicate additions, clarifications, or new subprocessors.
- Patch version changes, such as `1.0.1`, indicate corrections without material legal impact.
